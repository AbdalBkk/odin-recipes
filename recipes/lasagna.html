Lasagna

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious flavor, try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients you will need to make this recipe.

  • 800g of ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan and cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  1. Cook the meat: cook the meat until brown and scrumbly and add the onion, the garlic and the canned sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles: cook the noodles until al dente.
  3. Prepare the cheese layer: mix both cheese add the eggs, salt and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: layer the ingredients like so... Meat, noodles, cheese mix and repeat.
  5. Bake the lasagna: cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown
